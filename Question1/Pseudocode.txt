Creating a recipe book

Input:

-RecipeTitle as a string
-Ingredients as list of strings
-Steps as list of strings
-search ingredient as a string

Process:

1. Create a recipe book. 

2. Create a list of ingredients and a list of steps to prepare a certain recipe.

3. Add the recipe to the recipe book

4. If a user's inputted ingredient is in the recipe book display the corresponding recipe with its ingredients and steps in a list format.

5. Otherwise repeat step 4 until you match the searched ingredient with one of the ingredients in the recipe book. 


Output:

-Recipe as lists of steps and ingredients
